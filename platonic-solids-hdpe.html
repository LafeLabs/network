Platonic Solids

Platonic Solids are solids formed from regular polygons. There are exactly five of them. They are the tetrahedron, cube, octahedron, icosahedron and dodecahedron. We can fabricate them with sheets of thin HDPE from milk bottles, cutting out the patterns for them and welding the edges together with a candle and tweezers.
