Trash Robot's Mutual Aid Boards!

This page hosts the mutual aid boards of Trash Robot's personal network! What are those boards? People can give each other rides to get around the network and connect more people on the network. People can ask for supplies for mutual aid like food, water, sanitary supplies, tech supplies, medical supplies. People on the network can post ads for whatever their thing is on the ad board. The freestuff board is for people distributing free stuff, and is local to Denver or places you can get to on RTD. A newsboard can have announcements for the community.
